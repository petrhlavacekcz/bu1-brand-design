# BU1SPORT AI Design Pack

BU1SPORT is a sports e-commerce brand.

## Style

- strong
- high contrast
- product-first
- modern
- direct
- conversion-focused

## Colors

- black (#141414) base
- white (#FFFFFF) base
- pink (#d2147c) = action

## Rules

- pink = CTA, hover, active
- do not use pink as background everywhere
- product must always be clear

## CTA

- Add to cart = primary
- must be dominant
- pink

- express checkout = secondary
- must not compete

## Product card

Must show clearly:
- image
- title
- price

Not allowed:
- soft fashion style
- weak price visibility

## Layout

- clean grid
- clear hierarchy
- fast scanning

## Tone

- not luxury
- not soft
- not decorative

- strong
- clear
- performance-focused
